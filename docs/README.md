## Documentation Rules

- Do not claim production readiness.
- Do not use Debian/Kali/Ubuntu/vendor branding as product identity.
- Do not document fake screenshots or generated mockups as product proof.
- Do not include secrets, `.env` files, dumps, raw captures, or customer data.
- Mark roadmap items clearly.
